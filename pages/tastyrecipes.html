Project Details

I created this webpage for an online cookware eCommerce company, who wanted a subsite where their customers can share their favorite recipes they created using items purchased from the main site.

The front page shows the trending recipes based on customer feedback & recipe views. You can click the Chocolate Cheesecake image to see the recipe page.

I used Yellowtail font for the site name as it gives the effect of having been written with icing. I created the icons used for the dropdown menu when viewing in portrait with Figma. I used Flexbox in the header and recipe blogs, with the Favorite section set as the aside.
