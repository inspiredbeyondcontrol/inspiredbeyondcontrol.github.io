Hike With Me

Mobile App Concept

This is a mobile hike scheduling app concept. The goal is to provide users with the ability to:

  • Find a neary hiking trail, read reviews, see details like difficulty, etc.
  • Schedule a hike with a friend or join scheduled group hike
  • Get directions to desired trail
  • Share to social media
  • Keep track of hiking history
