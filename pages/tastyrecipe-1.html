Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients
  • Pie crust
  • Cream cheese (3 x 8 ounce packages)
  • Heavy cream
  • Powdered sugar (1 cup)
  • Vanilla extract (2 teaspoons)
  • Chocolate icing (for the topping)
  • Chocolate chips (for the cheesecake mixture)
Instructions

This is the no-bake recipe, but you can certainly do a water-bathed recipe if preferred. Here are the steps:

  1. In a bowl, use a whisk to beat the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. (or use a whisk beater if you have one)
  2. Remove the whipped cream from the mixing bowl and set aside.
  3. In the same mixing bowl, use a paddle to mix the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  4. Using a rubber spatula, fold in the whipped cream evenly, making sure to scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl completely.
  5. Spread the cheesecake filling into the crust and smooth with an off-set spatula.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.

Final step: ENJOY! (:
