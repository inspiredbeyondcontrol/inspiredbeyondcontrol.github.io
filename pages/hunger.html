Our Dream

Hunger No More has a dream - a world without hunger! We believe everyone shares in this dream. The great news is that you don't have to be a billionaire to join our fight! No matter how much you can give, or how often you can give it -- you are making this dream a reality!

Do you want to be on the front lines of this fight to END World Hunger? Scroll to the bottom and click the "Join Our Team!" button.

What We Do

We have bases all over the world, where we actively fight to end hunger by:

  • providing food & water to those in need
  • assisting local farms & markets to enable them to produce quality food for their communities

To vanquish world hunger, every community needs to be able to produce enough quality food for their families. Yet not all climates are the same, so we must do our due diligence to understand how best to accomplish our goal for each region. This is how we win the fight!

Our Accomplishments

We have delivered quality food to numerous communities in countries worldwide, including Africa, South America, and recently in Papua New Guinea. Additionally, we have revived dozens of family farms & local markets in these communities.

We are firm believers in the infamous proverb:

"Teach someone how to fish,
and they will never go hungry!"

We live this proverb every day!
